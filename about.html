About Me

Hi! I’m Rhythm. I am a rising junior at UC Davis, double majoring in Computer Science and Statistics. I have experience in full-stack development. I’ve worked heavily on the frontend and more recently gained experience working with the backend. I started programming in my freshman year of college. I didn’t enjoy it as much initially, but joining clubs was the deal breaker for me and made me stick through. I am so glad that I chose to stay in the field, as I’m more passionate than ever about creating impactful applications and products. I understand the importance of learning from real-world experiences and try to do that as much as possible. Feel free to reach out to me if you know of any opportunities where I might be a good fit!
